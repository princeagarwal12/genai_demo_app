How do I apply for a Wayfair Credit Card?
To learn about our Wayfair Credit Card or apply for a new account, visit the Wayfair Credit Card page.

How do I pay my Wayfair Credit Card bill or manage my account?
To pay your Wayfair Credit Card bill, change your banking information, and manage your personal information, log into your account.

How do I cancel my Wayfair Credit Card?
You can cancel your Wayfair Credit Card at any time by calling Citi at 800-365-2714, TTY: We accept 711 or other relay services.

By canceling, you won’t earn any new rewards, and you’ll lose any existing rewards associated with the account.
