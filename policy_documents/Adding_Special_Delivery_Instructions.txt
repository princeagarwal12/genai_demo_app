When can I add or update instructions?
Instructions can be added up to 48 hours prior to the day of delivery. Our delivery team will try their best to accommodate these changes.

What kind of information can I add to my delivery instructions?
Property type

Gate or door code

How many flights of stairs the delivery agent will need to go

Elevator availability

Package drop-off location

If a certificate of insurance (COI) is required

Point of contact’s name, phone number, and email

My building requires a Certificate of Insurance (COI). How far in advance do I need to request one?
Request a Certificate of Insurance at least three business days before your delivery date. You will be responsible for providing the certificate to your building.

Will updating instructions allow me to change my delivery method?
No, your delivery speed and method won’t be affected. Read our Shipping Information article to learn more about changing your service level.
